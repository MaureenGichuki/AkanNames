Akan names

Akan names come from a customary naming framework for people from nations like Togo, Ivory Coast and Ghana. A child's name is not set in stone by factors encompassing their introduction to the world. For instance, the day of their introduction to the world or the way in which they were conceived may figure out which name they get regardless of their orientation. These Akan names imply the day one is born.

Depending on gender and day of the week one can tell their Akan name by their birthday.
For males:
Sunday: Kwasi
Monday: Kwadwo
Tuesday: Kwabena
Wednesday: Kwaku
Thursday: Yaw
Friday: Kofi
Saturday: Kwame

For females:
Sunday: Akosua
Monday: Adwoa
Tuesday: Abenaa
Wednesday: Akua
Thursday: Yaa
Friday: Afua
Saturday: Ama
